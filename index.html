Understanding PCOS

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder common among women of reproductive age. Women with PCOS may have infrequent or prolonged menstrual periods or excess male hormone (androgen) levels.

Early detection is crucial for managing symptoms and preventing long-term complications. Our AI-powered detection tool analyzes X-ray images to identify potential indicators of PCOS.

Common Symptoms

  • Irregular menstrual cycles
  • Excess androgen levels
  • Polycystic ovaries
  • Weight gain
  • Skin issues

Lifestyle Changes

  • Regular exercise
  • Balanced nutrition
  • Weight management
  • Stress reduction

Medical Treatment

  • Hormone therapy
  • Ovulation medication
  • Anti-androgen medicines
  • Regular monitoring

Support System

  • Regular check-ups
  • Counseling services
  • Support groups
  • Family education
